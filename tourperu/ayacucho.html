Ancash inka route

3,100-m/10,000 ft above sea level. The Department of Ancash is located in northwest Peru and has the privilege to include in its territory all natural landscapes known in the world. In this city you can take wonderful tours ancash. To the west, the waters of the Pacific Ocean shore Ancash and to the east it is rimmed by the Andes Mountains. Ancash is home to more than 980,000 people. The capital is Huaraz, located at 3,100-m.a.s.l./10,000 ft. The coastal city of Chimbote is one of the world’s largest fishing ports and the capital of Peru’s iron and steel industry. Geographical diversity gives Ancash a varied weather that is warm on the coast but cold in the mountains with mild, crisp mornings all year round.
