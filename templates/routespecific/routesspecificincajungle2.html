Itinerary Cusco

INKA JUNGLE 4 DAYS AND 3 NIGHTS

TimeLine

In TourPeruTravellers, we offer you this magnificent inka route where you enjoy mountains, archaeological sites and more.

  • Inca Route: Start

    Our TourPeruTravellers Itinerary :

    1.- CUSCO – ABRA MALAGA – SANTA MARIA.

    2.- SANTA MARIA – SANTA TERESA.

    3.- SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    4.- AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Inca Route: Start

  • CUSCO – SANTA MARIA – SANTA TERESA.

    07:30 a.m. Pick up from the door of the office, we will depart in our transport towards the Sacred Valley of the Incas, where we will be able to appreciate beautiful and picturesque natural attractions. Arriving at the Malaga pass (4350 meters above sea level), there our guide will give you a safety talk, the starting point of our bicycle tour, we will find vegetation typical of the high jungle area, fruit fields and rivers. Arrival to Santa Maria. Our first night in basic lodging. In the afternoon OPTIONAL rafting.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    After breakfast we start walking along the banks of the Urubamba River. We find original Inca trails, trails full of vegetation, where we can appreciate the cultivation and harvest of coffee as well as the sacred plant of the Incas, La COCA, we will arrive at the small town of Quellomayo, rest and lunch. We continue until we reach the thermal baths of Santa Teresa after a relaxing bath we go to the town of Santa Teresa.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Breakfast, this day we walk to Aguas Calientes. The comfort of this day is in the pleasant climate that oscillates between 25-30 ° C. This one will be a bit different with five hours of walking, we will cross not only hanging bridges. Used by locals, but also oroyas which will make our day very interesting. Finally we arrive at the town of MACHUPICCHU Lodging in a very comfortable hostel.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Breakfast very early and then we go on foot to the citadel of Machupicchu, to be able to see the cloud forest and other natural wonders of dawn. We will practically have almost the whole day to visit the archaeological complex; They will have a guide for 2 hours and then free time. At the appropriate time, return to Aguas Calientes, to be able to board the train to Ollantaytambo. Pick up at the Ollantaytambo train station and transfer to the city of Cusco. 10:30 p.m. Arrival to the city of Cusco.

    Inca Route: Day 4
