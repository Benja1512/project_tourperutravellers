  • SACRED VALLEY.

    07:00 a.m. Breakfast, The bus will pick you up to start the full day excursion to visit the small town of Pisac as well as the ruins and the Indian market of Pisac. Then you will continue to enjoy the beautiful landscape of the Sacred Valley of the Incas until you reach the Fortress and small town of Ollantaytambo. Overnight in Aguas Calientes. Lunch. Including.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • MACHU PICCHU “WONDER OF THE WORLD”.

    06:00 am Breakfast at the hotel. At the appropriate time we will begin with the Excursion to the temple of Machu Picchu. Full day excursion that includes the guided visit (Guide only for 2 hours) In the afternoon return to the city of Cuzco and transfer to the Plaza de Cusco, return to the Hotel on behalf of the passenger (3 blocks from the hotel).

    Inca Route: Day 4

  • MOUNTAIN OF 7 COLORS.

    We begin with the pick up from your respective hotel at 4:30 am in the morning, we will go south of Cusco passing through the town of URCOS (along the road that leads Cusco-Puno) to the district of CHECACUPE (2 hours by bus ) then we will take the detour that will lead us to PITUMARCA and from where we will continue along a road passing through small towns until we reach the community of HANCHIPATA -QUESIUNO (1:20 hours by bus) the ideal place to start the walk, on the route We will appreciate many mountains as well as the snow-capped AUSANGATE, beautiful lagoons, grazing camelids. The walk will last approximately 3 hours until we reach EL CERRO DE COLORES, VINICUNCA OR MONTAÑA DE ARCO IRIS, in this place we will spend approximately one hour taking photos of the mountain and the beautiful landscape, then we will return to the starting point, the walk of Return will be approximately 3 hours to then return in our transport to Cusco. Return to the city of Cusco at approximately 7:00 pm. Lunch included. Does not include dinner.

    Inca Route: Day 5

  • JORGE CHAVEZ AIRPORT – LIMA.

    Transfer to Cusco Airport with return to Lima 2:20 p.m. The tourist bus will pass by your hotel to start the City Tour. City tours Modern Lima, Financial Center, Golf, Olivar, Huaca Pucllana, Lima the city of the kings; We will visit the Plaza San Martín, its balconies and old houses, the Plaza Mayor and its most important buildings such as the Government Palace, the Cathedral of Lima, the Archbishop's and Municipal Palace, the Desamparados Station, among others. We will enter one of the most important convents of the 17th century. and the underground crypts known as Catacombs. magic fountains.
