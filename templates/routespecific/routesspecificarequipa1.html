    Inca Route: Start

  • PAMPA CAÑAHUAS / NATIONAL RESERVE / CHIVAY.

    7:40 a.m. to 8:20 a.m. Pick up of passengers from your hotel located in the historic center Exit by the highway to Yura bordering the Chachani Volcano following the road we arrive at Pampa Cañahuas (National Reserve of Salinas and Aguada Blanca), view of South American camelids, Pampas de Toqra place of local birds and migratory, passing through Chucura, an extinct volcano, Patapampa and the Mirador de los Andes, a strategic point for viewing the volcanoes that are part of the Southern Cordillera of the Andes. 1:00 p.m. Approximate arrival to Chivay for buffet lunch (Optional) Installation in the intended housing In the afternoon, visit the thermal baths "La Calera" thermal medicinal water sources located 3 km from Chivay, it has facilities to take a relaxing bath. (bring swimwear). Dinner with folkloric show in Chivay restaurant (Optional) Lodging – Colca Valley

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • WARI QUINUA PAMPA DE AYACUCHO.

    breakfast first thing in the morning 6:15 a.m. approx. Departure to the town of Yanque, where we will have the opportunity to appreciate a brief show of typical dances of the area exhibited by children in its main square. The road continues to the viewpoint of the Cruz del Cóndor, after a stop and a light walk of 45 min. approx. from Cruz del Cura to Cruz del Cóndor. To appreciate the landscape that the valley offers, see in splendor the flight of the Condor and the depth of the Colca Canyon. On the return, I pass through the traditional towns of Pinchollo, Maca, Achoma, and Yanque, where we will observe stone models, hanging tombs, amphitheatres, churches, and agricultural terraces in the distance. We arrive at Chivay for the buffet lunch (Optional) Return to Arequipa. Approximate arrival at 16:30 hrs. Transfer of passengers to their respective accommodations.
