    Inca Route: Start

  • CUSCO – CALCA – LARES – WACAHUASI.

    early in the morning (5:00 am) we will pick you up from the hotel and begin the walk to the Valley of Lares, we will leave in the direction of Calca, crossing the popular Sacred Valley. After a few hours we will arrive at the town of Lares, where we will visit the thermal and medicinal baths. After the morning experience and lunch, we will begin our walk (approx. 3 hours and 30 min.) to the town of Wacawasi (first camp). Overnight, dinner and breakfast the next day.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • WACAWASI – IPSAYCOCHA LAGOON – PATACANCHA.

    After breakfast we will continue our walk and we will see the great skills of the inhabitants in regards to weaving and making garments; then we will go up to the Ipsaycocha pass (4100 m.s.n.m. or 13451 feet). From this place we will have the impressive view of the famous Veronica Mountain or Wakayhuillca (5750 m.s.n.m. or 18865 feet). After lunch we will continue descending for approximately an hour and a half towards our last camp which is located in the town of Patacancha.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • PATACANCHA – OLLANTAYTAMBO – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    Early, after breakfast we will leave in the direction of Ollantaytambo, walking downhill we will arrive at a viewpoint, which will give us an impressive view of the valley to reach the town of Huylloc, then we will continue walking to reach the town of Pumamarka and enjoy the Inca terraces, finishing the tour in Ollantaytambo, lunch. Then we will take the train to Machu Picchu Pueblo and spend the night in your reserved hostel.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – OLLANTAYTAMBO – CUSCO.

    Very early we will begin the trek towards Machupicchu and we will walk an average of 1h 30m along the Inca Trail to the control of Machu Picchu, we will get ready to enter, we will register and we will begin our guided tour for an average of two hours, within the Machu Picchu citadel. Then you will have free time to tour this impressive Inca city on your own, visiting the Huayna Picchu (previous payment and reservation), the Inti Punku and the Inca Bridge. Finally we end the day returning by train to Ollantaytambo and then our car to Cusco.
