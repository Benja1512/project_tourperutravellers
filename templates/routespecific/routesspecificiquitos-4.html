    Inca Route: Start

  • Iquitos – Cumaceba Amazon Lodge.

    Reception at the airport of the city of Iquitos. Short pedestrian city tour depending on the arrival time. Transfer to our private jetty to board our fast boat. Visit to the floating houses from November to July. Navigation through the Amazon River, observing its different hamlets. Typical drink upon arrival at Cumaceba Amazon Lodge. Allocation of private cabins and tour of the facilities. Walk through primary jungle knowing flora and fauna. Typical self-service lunch. In the afternoon tour of the Amazon River to search for gray and pink river dolphins. Visit to a riverside family where they preserve some species of animals such as anacondas, sloth bears, turtles, among others. Typical aphrodisiac drinks with medicinal power. Sunset over the Amazon. Night walk- insects, frogs, tarantulas etc. Dinner. Tales and myths of the jungle. Night at Cumaceba Amazon Lodge.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • Cumaceba Amazon Lodge

    Morning excursion and observation of birds in their habitat, knowing the legendary Shansho - Hoatzin. Breakfast. Visit the Yagua native group knowing their typical clothing, dialect, way of life. Demonstration with the blowpipe or pucuna how to hunt birds and small monkeys. Tourist Participation. Return to the Lodge and typical self-service lunch. Permanent guide. 3.00 PM return to the city of Iquitos, transfer to the hotel or airport.
