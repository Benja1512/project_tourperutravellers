Itinerary Cusco

CUSCO – MACHUPICCHU 4 DAYS AND 3 NIGHTS

TimeLine

In TourPeruTravellers, we offer you this magnificent inka route where u enjoy mountains, archaeological sites

    Inca Route: Start

  • PICK UP FROM THE AIRPORT + CITY TOUR.

    Pick up from Cusco Airport and transfer to the Hotel. At the appropriate time, the CITY TOUR will start, where we will visit the Archaeological Park of Sacsayhuaman, Qenqo, Pucapucara and Tambomachay (Approximate duration of 4 hours).

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • SACRED VALLEY OF THE INCAS.

    07:30 a.m. Start of the Sacred Valley of the Incas. On this tour we visit the typical market of Pisaq, the Archaeological Park of Pisaq, the town of Urubamba where we will have free time for lunch, then we will continue our tour visiting the archaeological complex of Ollantaytambo. Returning to Cusco, we will visit the Chinchero viewpoint.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • CUZCO - MACHUPICCHU – CUZCO.

    04:00 A.M.- Transfer to the train station to leave for the Sanctuary of Machupicchu. Full day excursion that includes a guided tour. In the afternoon return to the city of Cuzco and transfer to the Plaza de Cusco. From the Plaza to the hotel at the passenger's expense. Shared service.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • TRANSFER TO THE AIRPORT.

    At the appropriate time, transfer to the Cusco airport or bus station.

    Inca Route: Day 4
