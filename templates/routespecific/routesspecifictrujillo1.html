    Inca Route: Start

  • CSUN AND MOON HUACAS TOUR – CHAN CHAN – HUANCHACO.

    10:30 a.m. Guided visit to the Moche Countryside, Site Museum, sighting of the Huaca del Sol, which was the most influential religious center of the Mochica culture. Visit to the Huaca de la Luna, which is made up of several superimposed platforms, decorated with paintings and high reliefs with the main characters of Mochica iconography, such as the figure of the slaughtering God, Ai Apaec.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • CHAN-CHAN – HUANCHACO.

    2:30 p.m. The Tour begins at the Huaca Arco Iris, located in the Esperanza district, then we will visit the Tshudi or Nikan Palace, touring its environments. Finally we will enjoy Huanchaco, observing the "caballitos de totora, pier, artisan walk". 6:00 p.m. approx. end of the tour.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • CITY TOUR.
