Itinerary Cusco

INKA JUNGLE 3 DAYS AND 2 NIGHTS

TimeLine

In TourPeruTravellers, we offer you this magnificent inka route where you enjoy mountains, archaeological sites and more.

  • Inca Route: Start

    Our TourPeruTravellers Itinerary :

    1.- CUSCO – SANTA MARIA – SANTA TERESA.

    2.- SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    3.- AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Inca Route: Start

  • CUSCO – SANTA MARIA – SANTA TERESA.

    We pick you up from your hotel very early in the morning and we will board a bus to the Puerto Málaga pass, further down is the town of Alfamayo, from here we will begin the 3 to 4 hour bike ride to Santa María. To later take the bus to Santa Teresa to enjoy the thermal baths.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    At 7 am. We will start the second day of walking and we will finish it around 3 p.m. Arriving at Aguas Calientes. That night we will spend the night in a hostel with a private bathroom and hot water.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • AGUAS CALIENTES – MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Very early in the morning we will start the ascent to the citadel of Machu Picchu and we will have the opportunity to see the sunrise, then we will have a guided tour for 2 hours, then you can visit other places of interest. Later it will be the return to Cusco by train to the town of Ollantaytambo, where our bus awaits us to return to the city of Cusco.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • End of the Route
