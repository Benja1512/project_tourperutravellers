    Inca Route: Start

  • Iquitos – Cumaceba Amazon Lodge Reception at the airport of the city of Iquitos

    Reception at the airport of the city of Iquitos. Pedestrian City Tour. Depending on the time of arrival. Transfer to our private jetty to board our fast boat. Visit to the floating houses from November to July. Navigation through the Amazon River, observing its different hamlets. Typical drink upon arrival at Cumaceba Amazon Lodge. Allocation of private cabins and tour of the facilities. Typical self-service lunch. In the afternoon, walk through the primary forest knowing flora and fauna of the area. Depending on the weather conditions we will observe the sunset. Night walk observing insects, frogs, nocturnal tarantulas among others. Dinner. Amazon myths. Night in Cumaceba Amazon Lodge.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • Cumaceba Amazon Lodge

    Morning excursion and bird watching walking or in the lagoon, knowing the legendary Shansho - Hoatzin. Breakfast. Visit the Yagua native group to learn about their typical clothing, dialect, way of life, demonstration of the ancient way of hunting, with the "Blowgun", they make handicrafts that can be exchanged or bought. Typical self-ser vice lunch; tour of the Amazon river visiting a riverside family who preserve some varieties of animals such as: sloths, monkeys, turtles, "Anacondas" or boa constrictors. Search for gray and pink river dolphins. Night excursion in canoe, typical sounds of the humid tropical forest. Dinner. Night in Cumaceba Amazon Lodge.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • Cumaceba Amazon Lodge – Iquitos

    Dawn on the banks of the Amazon River watching birds on Padre Island by canoe. Canoe ride to appreciate the Victoria Regia, the largest aquatic plant in the world. Breakfast. Tour through the Amazon River to look for gray and pink river dolphins. Visit to a riverside family where they preserve some species of animals such as anacondas, lazy bears, turtles, among others. Typical aphrodisiac drinks with medicinal power. Lunch. Relax in the hammock house. 3:00 pm return to the city of Iquitos, transfer to the hotel or airport.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • Cumaceba Amazon Lodge - Recreational Fishing – Iquitos

    Breakfast and observation of the largest Aquatic Lilac in the world, La Victoria. Regia, Recreational fishing excursion species such as: catfish, sardines and voracious "Piranhas". Typical self-service lunch. Opportune time return to the city of Iquitos, transfer to the hotel or airport.
