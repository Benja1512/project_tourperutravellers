Ayacucho located a height 2761msnm 03:00 PM: In the afternoon, visit the colonial city where we can appreciate the Baroque-style architecture that is characterized by its stone arches that surround the four sides of the main square, the Casona Boza y Solís, Casona Cristóbal Castilla y Zamora among others, the archaeological museum known as Hipólito Unanue, the Acuhcimay Viewpoint, the Chapel of the Monastery of Santa Teresa to enjoy the sweets prepared by the mothers of the Discalced Carmelites, we will visit the most representative artisan workshops of the city such as Huamanga stone, Altarpiece , textiles and finally the Cathedral.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • WARI QUINUA PAMPA DE AYACUCHO.

    09:30 A: M. Departure to the Wari archaeological complex, the capital of the First Andean Empire (Pre-Inca culture), visit the Site Museum, the Templo Mayor, the underground chambers of the Monjachayuq and Cheqowasi neighborhoods. Then we will visit the artisan workshop of the masters who will teach us the entire ceramic process in the town of Quinua, a typical place that has narrow cobbled streets and tile roofs that give a peculiar attractive and cozy appearance that is characterized by being clearly Potter. Just one kilometer from Quinua is the Pampa de Ayacucho Historical Sanctuary, where visitors will be able to see the scene where the Battle of Ayacucho took place, an event that sealed the independence of Peru and America, where we will observe the monument of the freedom represented by the 44m high Obelisk. It is located at a height of 3300 meters above sea level.
