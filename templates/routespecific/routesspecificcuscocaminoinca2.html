    Inca Route: Start

  • CUSCO – WAYLLABAMBA – LLULLUCHAPAMPA.

    Collect from the passengers of their respective lodging (6:00 am.), In a private bus, and after crossing the towns of Chinchero, Urubamba and Ollantaytambo in "Sacred Valley of the Incas" in the direction of El Km. 82 (Piscakuchocho ), Then we will start our walk, stop at Miskay, we restart the walk towards our first camp in Wayllabamba to Almorz, in the afternoon we will pass through the first open and camp in our first camp Llulluchapampa.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • LLULLUCHAPAMPA – SAYACMARCA – CHAQUICOCHA.

    After breakfast we will start the walk towards the highest point of the Inca road, where we can observe different microclimates. Passing through the Warmiwañusca Abra at 4,200 m.s.n.m. We will have lunch in Pacaymayu, we will visit the Runkuracay complex continuing with the walk we will descend towards Sayacmarca that is a semicircular construction at different levels, then approximately with a 20 -minute walk we will arrive at the Chaquicocha camp.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • LLULLUCHAPAMPA – SAYACMARCA – CHAQUICOCHA.

    We continue the walk after having breakfast towards the third pass (3650m) to continue towards the Phuyupatamarca Complex where you can see the Urubamba Valley and finally Wiñayhuayna (2650m) last camp.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • WIÑAYHUAYNA – MACHUPICCHU – CUSCO.

    After breakfast, at approximately 4:30 a.m. we will start the walk towards the Puerta del Sol (Inti Putu), where we will be able to appreciate the majestic and imposing Citadel of Machupicchu. Arriving at Machupicchu, guided tour and free time, then we descend to the town of Aguas Calientes, where you can visit the thermal baths, finally we board the train to return to the City of Cusco.

    Inca Route: Day 4
