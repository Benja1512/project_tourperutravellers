    07:30 am Pick up from the hotel and transfer to the port of Puno. Then you visit 2 different islands in the Uros for an average of 1 hour 30 minutes located 6 km from the lake port with an altitude of 3812 meters above sea level, the Uros are islands built with totora reeds, the same ones that are used to build their houses. , boats, even use it for food and fuel. Approximately 1:00 p.m. We will arrive at the island of Amantani where we will have a warm reception by the population, the distribution of the passengers is made by the guide in family homes where lunch and dinner and breakfast the next day are included. . The meals are prepared based on local products. At night, families hold a welcome reception for new visitors with folk music.
