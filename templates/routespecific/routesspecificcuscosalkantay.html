Itinerary Cusco

SALKANTAY 5 DAYS AND 4 NIGHTS

TimeLine

In TourPeruTravellers, we offer you this magnificent inka route where u enjoy mountains, archaeological sites

  • Inca Route: Start

    Our TourPeruTravellers Itinerary :

    1.- CUSCO – SORAYPAMPA (15 KM APPROX).

    2.- SORAYPAMPA – CHAULLAY (21KM APPROX).

    3.- CHAULLAY – SAHUAYACO BEACH – SANTA TERESA (11KM APPROX).

    4.- SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES (17 km approx).

    5.- MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    Inca Route: Start

  • CUSCO – SORAYPAMPA (15 KM APPROX).

    5:00 a.m. We leave from Cusco, passing through the towns of Izcuchaca and Limatambo arriving at Mollepata (2900 m.s.n.m) where we will be at approximately 8:30 a.m. where we will have our breakfast (not included), then we will prepare to start our excursion first we will take a car that will take us to the point of markokasa (50 min) (3380m.s.n.m) from that point we will begin our walk in approximately 5 hours passing through the towns of markokasa and canaleta inka and thus arriving at soraypamapa (3900m .a.s.n.m) where we will be arriving at 1:30 p.m. there we will have our first lunch and first camp after lunch we can go to visit the beautiful Humantay lagoon (4200 m.s.n.m) 1.30 hours up where we will have impressive views of said mountain and at night We will have our dinner and then we can rest.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • SORAYPAMPA – CHAULLAY (21KM APPROX)

    Being 7:00 am we will have to taste a nutritious breakfast and then continue with our walk, we start from the point of soraypamapa to the highest point open salkantay (5 hours) (4650m.s.n.m) passing through salkantaypampa and soyrococha where we will appreciate a small lagoon then we will begin the descent to huayracmachay (3h) (3800m.s.n.m) where we will have our lunch then we will continue descending to chaullay (2h) (2900m.s.n.m) where it will be our second camp.

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • CHAULLAY – SAHUAYACO BEACH – SANTA TERESA (11KM APPROX).

    Continuing our walk after a comforting breakfast we will begin our walk appreciating a variety of coca crops, considered the sacred leaf of our ancestors which is currently cultivated in tropical areas, this last section will be descending to Playa (5h) (1900m.s.n.m ) where we will have our lunch and from there we will head to Santa Teresa on a bus where we will spend the night. Optional visit the Cocalmayo hot springs.

    Inca Route: Day 3

  • SANTA TERESA – AGUAS CALIENTES (17 km approx).

    The sunrise will be within a tropical area full of history and nature, where our breakfast awaits us ready to taste. Arriving at this town will take us approximately 6 hours, passing through the well-known rest center of Hidroeléctrica in this Puno we will take a box lunch (Hydroelectric Train Service – Aguas Calientes: Optional), making stops in different places to simply appreciate the landscape and its surroundings full of waterfalls with impressive falls which come from the glaciers, arriving in the afternoon at the mentioned town that is popularly known as "hot waters", (2000m.s.n.m) there we will spend the night in a hostel at the foot of the Inka citadel of Machupicchu. We will have our last dinner in a restaurant and our guide will organize everything for the next day.

    Inca Route: Day 4

  • MACHU PICCHU – CUSCO.

    At dawn around 04:30 in the morning we will ascend towards the majestic Inka city of Machupicchu where we will visit accompanied by our guide for approximately 2 hours, and the rest of the day free in which you can visit optional (it is not included in the package) Huayna Picchu or Machu Picchu mountain once again the entire citadel, we return in the afternoon to the town of Machu Picchu town depending on the schedule of our return train which we will announce once you book your trip with our company. Pick up at the Ollantaytambo train station and transfer to the city of Cusco.

    Inca Route: Day 5
