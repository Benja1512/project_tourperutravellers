    Inca Route: Start

  • LIMA – CUZCO.

    Reception at the Cuzco airport and transfer to the chosen hotel.

    Inca Route: Day 1

  • CUSCO – KM.104 – CHACHABAMBA – WIÑAYHUAYNA – AGUAS CALIENTES.

    I collect from the passengers of their respective lodges of 5:30 a.m. at 6:00 a.m., to go to the train station, where we start to km. 104, place that we will give the beginning of our walk after approximately 4 hours of walking, during which lunch is given, then we continue towards the Wiñayhuayna complex, guided tour, we continue the walk to the citadel of Machupicchu, then we descend by bus to the people of Aguas Calientes, where they spend the night (lodging).

    Inca Route: Day 2

  • CAGUAS CALIENTES- MACHUPICCHU – CUSCO.

    Breakfast, ascent to Machupicchu, guided tour, at the end of it will have a free time. Then we will descend to the town of Aguas Calientes, where you can visit the thermal baths, return to the city of Cusco by train.
