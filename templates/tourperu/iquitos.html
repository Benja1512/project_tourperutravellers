Iquitos, nestled in the heart of the Peruvian Amazon, is known as the "Gateway to the Jungle." This vibrant city offers a unique blend of rainforest adventure, indigenous culture, and rich biodiversity. As a key entry point to explore the Amazon River and its surrounding jungle, Iquitos is home to exotic wildlife, floating villages, and fascinating eco-lodges.

On your trip to Iquitos, you can experience a wide range of exciting activities that immerse you in the natural beauty and cultural richness of the Amazon:

  • Amazon River Cruises – Sail through the mighty Amazon River and explore its diverse ecosystems. Enjoy sightings of pink river dolphins and gray dolphins, and visit indigenous communities along the riverbanks.
  • Jungle Trekking – Explore the lush Amazon rainforest on guided treks, where you can discover exotic wildlife, medicinal plants, and unique ecosystems. Jungle walks can include day trips or night excursions to observe nocturnal creatures like tarantulas, frogs, and insects.
  • Visit Indigenous Communities – Learn about the cultures and traditions of local indigenous groups, such as the Yagua people. Participate in cultural demonstrations, see traditional dances, and try using a blowgun.

Iquitos offers a unique gateway to explore the wonders of the Amazon, providing thrilling outdoor activities, rich wildlife encounters, and cultural insights into the heart of the rainforest.
