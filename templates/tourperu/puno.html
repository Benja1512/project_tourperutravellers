The Titicaca Lake tour takes place on Lake Titicaca, located in the Puno region of southern Peru. Lake Titicaca is the highest navigable lake in the world, situated at an altitude of 3,812 meters (12,507 feet) above sea level, on the border between Peru and Bolivia. The tour specifically visits the Uros Islands, Amantani Island, and Taquile Island, all within the Peruvian side of the lake.

On your trip to Peru, the Puno - Lake Titicaca tour offers an immersive blend of culture, nature, and unforgettable landscapes:

  • Uros Floating Islands – Visit the unique floating islands of Uros, where the locals have created homes from totora reeds, and learn about their way of life.
  • Amantani Island – Stay with local families on Amantani Island, experiencing authentic hospitality, traditional food, and folk music under the stars.
  • Taquile Island – Explore the serene beauty of Taquile Island, enjoy stunning views of the lake, and discover its rich cultural heritage and handwoven textiles.
